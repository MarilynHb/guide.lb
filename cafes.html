CAFES | REVIEWS

BEYT 📍 MAR MIKHAEL


This hidden Cafe in Mar Mikhail stole my heart. As soon as you enter the place you directly fall in love with their spacious rooms and interior. Everything is just so clean, modern and artistic. Wait till you see the garden, it’s even better! It so cute and has a lot of greenery, making you feel like you teleported in 1 minute from Mar Mikhail to some tropical island🌿

On Sundays they do Brunch buffet. But I haven’t tried it yet☺️ A perfect place to relax, study, work, read a book📚 Or meet up with a friend ☺️ Very welcoming staff too. But service is very slow.
🐶Pet friendly
💞Rate: 9/10







KALEI 📍HAMRA


This hidden coffeeshop is an ideal place for someone looking to escape busy streets of Beirut while savoring Lebanese architecture of the old yet renovated traditional house with vintage as well as modern spirit. You can lay foundation of your experience inside the house, on the terrace or in the garden🌿 I loved that their garden is spacious and there is a good social distancing. 👎🏻The service could have been better. It was very slow. But we weren’t in a rush, so it didn’t bother us that much.

👥Service /10 🍽Food 8/10 ✨Atmosphere 9,5/10
💦Cleanliness 9/10 💸Affordable 10/10
💕Overall: 8,5/10 🐶Pet friendly







BACKBURNER 📍 SAIFI VILLAGE


One of my most favorite coffees ☕️ in town. I love to get caffeinated from this place on a sunny day ☀️ sit outside or just take away the coffee and go for a walk around beautiful Saifi. You can visit the coffeeshop just to drink coffee or tea, for breakfast or for lunch because they offer some salads 🥙 too and sandwiches 🥪 I like the salmon sandwich the most, along with their cakes. 👎🏻One of the negative things about the place is that it’s pretty small and the seatings are not extremely cozy. And it’s one of the most priciest coffeeshops in town. But the coffee is great quality so was never disappointed.

👥Service 8/10 🍽Food 7,5/10 ✨Atmosphere 7,5/10
💦Cleanliness 8,5/10 💸Affordable 6,5/10 💕Overall: 7,6/10 🐶Pet friendly
