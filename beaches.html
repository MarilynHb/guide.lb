BEACHES | REVIEWS

JAMMAL📍 BATROUN


If you are looking for a really good fish right by the sea 🌊 or in this place’s case literally in the sea, then you should check this absolutely beautiful hidden gem in Batroun. We went there on Monday last week, it was empty, but at the same time great! All beach just for ourselves. Yes last week the weather was still amazing and we had a wonderful swim. One of the downsides of the place is the prices. The fish is pricey. But you pay for the place’s location, private beach access and the freshest fish 🎣 so in the end it’s worthy. Service was good. But then again we were only table ordering food, so don’t know how it is on a crowded day.

👥Service 8/10 ✨Atmosphere 9/10
💦Cleanliness 8/10 🧡 Food 8/10 💸Affordable 6/10
💕Overall: 7,8/10 🐶Pet friendly







PIERRE AND FRIENDS 📍 THOUM


Looking for a prefect spot to meet sunset 🌅 by the sea? This place won’t disappoint you. You can visit Pierre & Friends during the day for lunch, swim, sunset drinks, dinner or even party! I personally prefer to go there for sunset drinks 🍹 or dinner 🍽 The feeling of seating right by the sea🌊 listening to the waves and cool music makes you forget you are even in Lebanon. I felt as If I’ve traveled to the Caribbeans🌴 On top of the nice views, ambiance and cool location, the food is also delicious!

👥Service 8/10 🍽Food 8/10 ✨Atmosphere 8,5/10
💦Cleanliness 7,5/10 💸Affordable 8/10
💕Overall: 8/10 🐶Pet friendly







KEMPINSKI 📍SUMMERLAND


Things I really love about Kempinski is its location, interior, spacious rooms, huge pools and always vacay vibes🌴 The pools are really awesome ! 💦 They are big and clean, and during the week there were not so many people, so it was extremely relaxing. The service at Summerland (the pool) is as slow as it always is, I already complained about it many times … so nothing new😁 You can enter it without having to stay at the hotel. But food this year at the pool was much better👌🏻We tried Barbecue Salad and Grilled chicken breast and It was better than expected. Breakfast Is not included in the room. We ordered room service and had it on the balcony with a wonderful view😍 I loved the Oriental set, It’s extremely filling and is more than enough for 2 people👍🏻 I think it’s a great destination for a romantic👩‍❤️‍👨 or family getaway 👨‍👩‍👦‍👦 and currently one of the best resorts in Lebanon.
I hope they would still work on improving their service and some little mistakes though.

👥Service 7,5/10 🍽Food 7,5/10 ✨Atmosphere 8/10
💦Cleanliness 9/10 💸Affordable 6/10 💕Overall: 7,6/10
