ROOFTOPS | REVIEWS

ALBERGO 📍 MONOT


You can pick to sit on the rooftop with the pool, or on the smaller rooftop one floor below. I picked the smaller rooftop because the atmosphere there is more cozy and the chairs are more comfortable. I love that not only you get to see a nice view, but you are also surrounded by a lot of greenery and the lighting is done so nicely, creating a very charming ambiance 😍 Very nice music on the background. Romantic but also lively.

So yes I honestly really enjoyed this romantic dinner experience, the atmosphere, the food… it was just PERFECTLY SPLENDID 😄❤️

🔸When to go: Romantic dinner/family dinner/ double date/anniversary/birthday
💗Overall experience: 8/10

KALEI 📍HAMRA


This hidden coffeeshop is an ideal place for someone looking to escape busy streets of Beirut while savoring Lebanese architecture of the old yet renovated traditional house with vintage as well as modern spirit. You can lay foundation of your experience inside the house, on the terrace or in the garden🌿 I loved that their garden is spacious and there is a good social distancing. 👎🏻The service could have been better. It was very slow. But we weren’t in a rush, so it didn’t bother us that much.

👥Service /10 🍽Food 8/10 ✨Atmosphere 9,5/10
💦Cleanliness 9/10 💸Affordable 10/10
💕Overall: 8,5/10 🐶Pet friendly


CLAP 📍BEIRUT


Finally one of the best rooftops in Beirut has opened its doors again this year.
Clap is a high-end restaurant that serves contemporary Japanese cuisine 🍣
Here you can find lively vibes, ritzy design and Dj Playing trendy House music all night while you dine. Basically the Zuma of Lebanon. Yes the place is pricey, but the ingredients are good quality, something very important in Lebanon these days. I personally love their sushi. The rooftop is also fully booked everyday 🙈 so make sure to book a week before your table. Overall, even though there were some mistakes in service, we still enjoyed our dinner and would come back, as we love the vibes of this rooftop.

🔸When to go: omantic dinner/ Dinner with friends/Birthday or Anniversary dinner 💕Overall: 8/10
